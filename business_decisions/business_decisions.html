Making Business Decisions Based on Data Project¶

Together with the marketing department, we've compiled a list of hypotheses that may help boost revenue. We will prioritize these hypotheses, launch an A/B test, and analyze the results.
We will work with 3 dataframes: hypotheses, orders and visits in order to reach the conclusion about the test. We will apply ICE and RICE frameworks in order to decide which hypothesis should be prioritized and tested on the users' groups.
We will aggrigate orders and visits dataframes in order to measure the statistical significance in the gropups' conversion rates and average order sizes. Also we will measure other metrics as cumulative revenue, order size of the groups.
Analyzing the results of the A/B test we will decide whether the test was successfull or not and whether it is worth continueing after the first month.

9. Find the statistical significance of the difference in conversion between the groups using the raw data. Make conclusions and conjectures. ¶

Hypothesis:
H0: there's not a statistically significant difference in conversion between the groups A and B.
H1: average conversion level differs between the groups A and B.

A critical statistical significance level will be set at 0.05 as it is a commonly accepted one in the industry.
